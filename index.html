Lavander Werdi

    • Hello everybody! I am a creative. I can never seem to stop creating anything. There is always an idea, new picture, or some business idea running through my head.

      Hello! Lavander Werdi is my nickname. I am an artist based in New York and im study web design at this moment.Design is my life. Art means a lot to me. I go to all the exhibitions of familiar artists and also do my own. For me, color is important, the combination of bright colors is my concept. I have no single definition for my works. I cannot clearly distinguish one direction or another in my work. In each of my paintings there are both real, and unreal, and abstract, and conditional, that is, a certain symbiosis of all that is in painting. But, of course, symbols and images prevail, through which I want to convey to the viewer the feelings and idea of ​​what I came to and thought about.
